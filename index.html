Priyanka mohan

Priyanka Arul Mohan Known as Priyanka Mohan, is an Indian actress who primarily appears in Tamil and Telugu films. She made her acting debut with the Kannada film Ondh Kathe Hella, and played the leading lady in the Telugu film Gang Leader and the Tamil films Doctor, Don and Etharkkum Thunindhavan.
